What Aptron does

Aptron is a private, single-owner dashboard for organizing your day. You create time-blocks and goals on a calendar, and Aptron mirrors them to and from your Google Calendar so your planning stays consistent across both. It also includes lightweight trackers for workouts, wardrobe, and daily health — all tied to one account.

How Aptron uses your Google Calendar

Aptron only touches your calendar after you explicitly connect it from the dashboard. When connected, Aptron:

• reads events from your Google Calendar to show them alongside your Aptron blocks, and
• writes the blocks you create in Aptron back to your calendar, keeping the two in sync.

Aptron never sells or shares your calendar data. You can disconnect at any time from the dashboard — which stops syncing and revokes Aptron's access — or remove it from your Google account permissions. See our Privacy Policy for details.
